Hello
My name is Tzadok, my love story of watch and numismatics collecting started at 2010.
After 3 years of military service I hand over a 82's Rolex two tone by my grandfather(which he's a watch collector by himself). I started to wear watches and my curiosity and passion started to grown.

During my work around Europe at 2010 after the service I noticed the beauty of the diffrent coins and banknotes.
At this point I decided to take my small numismatics collection to the next step and all banknotes and coins in my collection include only in countries Iv'e been or people I know personaly been or have a conection to these country.


Now days my collection contain over 150 watches and over 300 banknotes and coins from over 170 countries and terretories.
